about us

We are a friendly digital factory with an emphasis on great design and user experience. We love to combine the newest technologies with fresh business models and help our clients monetize from them. In this fast-paced digital era we help you plan, innovate, develop your brand and online presence which will get you noticed and help you convert visitors to clients. No job is too small or big for us, we understand the different needs of people starting businesses and big corporations alike. Our services range from branding and design via development through to business strategy, check out the full-list of our services below. We mainly traverse London (GMT) and Helsinki (GMT+2) timezones but are happy to adapt to the timing needs of global customers.
